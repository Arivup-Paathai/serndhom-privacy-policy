Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Serndhom Matrimony account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  • Open the Serndhom Matrimony app
  • Go to Settings
  • Click on "Request Account Deletion"
  • Your profile will be hidden immediately
  • Permanent deletion will occur within 7 days

Option 2: Email Request

You may also request account deletion by emailing us at: serndhommtrimony@gmail.com

Data Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Uploaded images
  • Account authentication data

Some data may be retained if required by law.
