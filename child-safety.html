Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: February 2026

Serndhom Matrimony is committed to protecting users from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that involves minors in sexual or exploitative activities.

Our Policies

  • Users must be 18 years or older to create an account.
  • Any profile found misrepresenting age will be permanently removed.
  • Sharing of inappropriate, illegal, or exploitative content is strictly prohibited.
  • We actively monitor and review reported content.

Reporting Mechanism

Users can report profiles directly inside the app using the "Report" feature.

Action Taken

  • Immediate account suspension upon verified violation.
  • Permanent ban for severe violations.
  • Cooperation with law enforcement when required.
